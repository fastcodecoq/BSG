Our Services


We provide broadcast and video production services worldwide including:

Turn Key packaging of Multi-Camera Remote Productions

BSG is able to be a turn key solution for all of your production needs. We are here for you every step of the way from the beginning stages of budgeting and planning. Followed by securing the best facilities and crew for your project. Plus we provide production and technical management on site.

HD, SD, and 3D Mobile Video Production Trucks

BSG supplies mobile production trucks nationwide and beyond. From 53' Double Expando units with full B-Unit for support, to small Production/Uplink combo units. We've got you covered.

Qualified Television and Event Production Crews Nationwide

In the event that your next production is in a major market or rural area, BSG is your one stop shop to handle your crew (union and non-union) and payroll concerns. We strive to give you the most qualified crew for your job and we'll let you know right away of any local shortages due to a busy market so we can plan accordingly.


ENG Packages Nationwide

We've got you covered anywhere in the US whether you need a one man "run & gun" videographer with simple audio and lighting needs, or if you need multiple shooters and an audio engineer. Our shooters are ready to help with a number camera types/formats, and can even take you to one of our affiliate studios if you need to do a feed out.

Satellite Transmission Services

BSG provides C band and Ku Band remote transmission services. We can help you with the simplest C Band Uplink and satellite space booking, or we can help when you need your uplink truck to have production capabilities for a live hit or small Split-Feed Production. We can also take care of your fiber transmission needs.

Webcasting and Video Streaming of Live Events and Conferences

Webcasting and streaming makeup one of the fastest growing areas of video production. We can help with your single camera corporate announcement, multi-location videoconference, or the most commonly seen live webcast of an event that is live to tape for a delayed broadcast. The most important thing is that the same level of production value is delivered owing to the fact that this is a different method of transmission.




We are now the exclusive rental house for Orbiter low profile camera platforms in the United States.

The Orbiter 500 superflat is a low profile camera seat and has been designed for all low shooting camera positions at outdoor broadcasts such as soccer, tennis or athletics but also for studio use. We currently have the 500 Superflats, and will have the new 750 Hybrids at the end of the year.
